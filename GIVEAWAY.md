# Grokar Car Giveaway

## Giving Back This Christmas Season

We're excited to announce that **all creator fees collected by Grokar are being donated to give away a car** to one lucky winner this holiday season.

---

### Why We're Doing This

At Grokar, we believe in giving back to our community. Car troubles can be stressful and expensive, especially during the holidays when families need reliable transportation the most. This Christmas, we want to make a real difference in someone's life.

Every diagnostic, every VIN decode, every piece of advice you receive through Grokar contributes to this goal. **100% of our creator fees go directly toward purchasing a vehicle for our giveaway winner.**

---

### How to Enter

1. Visit [Grokar](/) and click the **"Enter Now"** button on the giveaway banner
2. Fill in your details:
   - Full Name
   - X (Twitter) Username
   - Date of Birth (must be 18+)
3. Accept the terms and submit your entry
4. That's it! You're entered to win

### Giveaway Details

- **Prize**: A brand new car
- **Eligibility**: Must be 18 years or older
- **Entry**: One entry per person (verified by X username)
- **Drawing**: Winner will be announced on our X (Twitter) account
